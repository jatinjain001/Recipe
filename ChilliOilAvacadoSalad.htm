Recipe Servings: 2 Pre Time:10 min

Cook Time:15 min Total Time:25 min


Ingredients Of Herbs And Paprika Chicken Breast


  • Delicut's Herb
  • Paprika Chicken Breast
  • Tortillas
  • Mayonaise
  • Cheese
  • Thinly sliced vegetables

    • How to Make Herbs and Paprika Chicken Breast


    1.Firstly, thaw the chicken breast in lukewarm water.

    2.Pan sear it for two minutes and dice it into thin slices.

    3.Now grab a few tortillas and place the diced slices into them, evenly. or you can enjoy it as a standalone recipe too. The choice is yours!

    4.Finish off the dish by garnishing with cheese, mayo and any veggies of your choice. Your delicious meal is ready to eat!
